Overview

What We've Built

The Construction Business Blueprint portal is your private online platform for managing your mentoring clients. Everything lives in one place -- business plans, training content, weekly check-ins, and AI coaching. Your clients log in, do their work, and stay accountable. You see exactly who's on track and who needs a push, without chasing anyone.

It works on any device -- laptop, tablet, or phone. Clients can install it as an app directly from their browser, no App Store needed. Push notifications handle the reminders for you.

The Process

How It Works - Client Journey

From invite to ongoing management, this is the full loop.

1
You Invite a Client Enter their name and email -- they receive an invite to join
2
They Set Up Their Account Create a login, add their business details, and they're in
3
You Create Their Business Plan Set up phases, milestones, and action items tailored to that client
4
They Work Through Plan + Training Ticking off actions, watching modules, making progress each week
5
Weekly Check-In (Automatic Reminders) They share wins, challenges, mood, and questions -- push notifications chase them, not you
6
You Review and Reply See every check-in on your dashboard, reply directly
7
Traffic Lights Flag Problems Early Anyone falling behind is automatically highlighted -- no one slips through
Built-In Intelligence

Smart Features

Green - On Track Check-ins up to date, actively engaged
Amber - Needs Attention Check-in overdue by 7+ days
Red - At Risk Login 10+ days or check-in 14+ days overdue

Traffic Light System

Every client gets a green, amber, or red status based on their engagement. Instantly see who's on track and who needs a nudge.

Automatic Check-In Reminders

Push notifications go out on check-in day to any client who hasn't submitted. The system chases them so you don't have to.

Blueprint AI Coaching

An AI assistant available to both you and your clients. Gives tailored business advice based on each client's situation and goals.

Private Internal Notes

Add notes on any client that only you can see. Track observations, follow-up items, or anything you want to remember.

Push Notifications

Clients get reminders directly to their phone. Check-in day alerts, new content notifications, and event reminders.
